Developing a B2B Online Platform For Barbers and Salons Within Quezon Avenue

Objectives

  • To expand their unison services towards isolated customers.
  • Provide haircuts to those who can't go outside.
  • To help barbers go back to businesses that were affected by the pandemic.
  • To ensure the community will benefit and profit from the web application.
  • To pave the way for future app developers to reach out to local businesses.
