About Us

We decided to conduct this study as a way to help local businesses, specifically barbers and salons, that lost business opportunities due to the pandemic. We aim to create a web application that will become a platform for barbers and salons residing on Quezon Avenue. Customers will be able to book an appointment online through the application. In this way, the barbers will have a flexible schedule.
